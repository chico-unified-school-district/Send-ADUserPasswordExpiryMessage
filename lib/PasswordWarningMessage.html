It is that time again. Change your password when prompted and share this information as needed.

You must change your password not less than every six months.

The new password needs to be a minimum of 12 characters, but we recommend using 16 characters.

  • Include 3 of these 4 items:
    • At least 1 UPPERCASE letter
    • At least 1 lowercase letter
    • At least 1 number
    • At least 1 special character
Instructions for changing your password:

  • After you login to your district Windows computer press CTRL+ALT+DEL

  • Select Change a Password option and follow the instructions.

  • Enter your old password, then enter your new password, and enter it again in the 'confirm' password field
